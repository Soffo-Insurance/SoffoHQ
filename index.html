Built on public, inspectable sources

Soffo works from materials firms use in intake: policies, case law, state rules, weather data, inspections, and supporting claim documents. The system depends on public, verifiable information, not hidden know-how.

Reasoning you can inspect

Soffo is not a black-box AI. It shows the documents, evidence, and legal support behind its reasoning, so firms can see why a conclusion was reached instead of just receiving an unexplained answer.

Judgment support, not judgment replacement

Soffo does not make the final intake decision for the firm. It helps attorneys make that decision by showing evidence strength, viable rebuttal paths, and the level of legal support behind each path.
